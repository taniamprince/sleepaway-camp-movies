April 29, 2003

We've got some Great News for Aunt Martha fans tonight! After an almost 2-decade long hiatus from acting, SHE'S COMING BACK- No Strings attached!!! Joining into the cast which includes SLEEPAWAY'S killer camper FELISSA ROSE in the upcoming flick: UNDER SURVEILLANCE, DESIREE plays the pain-killer addicted mother of the movie's main character. sleepawaycampmovies.com's JEFF HAYES also makes a cameo in the film and shot some behind the scenes photos of DESIREE prepping and rehearsing for one of her scenes.

She was one of the craziest AUNTS ever to grace the silver screen! She gave a performance that people will never forget...and then...she vanished! Desiree Gould hasn't appeared in a movie in about 2 decades. SO you thought it was all "Water Under The Bridge" did ya?? THINK AGAIN! She re-emerged into the public eye at the SLEEPAWAY CAMP REUNION in 2001...and now she's ready to take your breath away once again...
