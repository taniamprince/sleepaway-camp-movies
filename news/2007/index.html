April 10, 2007

MEET "RICKY" AT HORRORFEST!

On Saturday April 21, Jonathan Tiersten will be meeting fans and signing autographs between 1:00 and 2:00. Then at 2:00 it's the MidWest Premiere Screening of the slasher-comedy short GRANDMA'S SLOPPY SECONDS featuring roles by Sleepaway Camp III's Daryl "Riff" Wilcher and original "Angela" Felissa Rose. Run time is 42 minutes. Then between 4-5pm, Jonathan will be doing a Q&A to answer all your questions about "Sleepaway Camp" and SOME of your questions about "Return To Sleepaway Camp" complete with a behind-the-scenes sneak-peek!

At 8pm Saturday Night, fans will also be treated to an acoustic performance by "Ricky" himself. Jonathan will be meeting fans again on Sunday, April 22 between 1 and 2pm.

  • RTSC FAQ's Here you will find answers to many of the most frequently asked questions about this movie.
