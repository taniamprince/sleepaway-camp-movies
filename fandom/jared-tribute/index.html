Jared's Sleepaway Camp Tribute

by Jared Hall


My name is Jared and I am 14. When I was 13 I went to a video store and was looking at the horror movies when I saw a movie with a knive through a sneaker. I asked my mom if I could get it and she said "yes". When I got home I watched SLEEPAWAY CAMP and loved it! When I got done watching it I watched it with the commentary. Thats when I found out about sleepawaycampmovies.com. When I got on the internet I went to the web site and looked at everything. Now I got all the SLEEPAWAY CAMP films on VHS and DVD, but the original SLEEPAWAY CAMP is my favorite movie of all time.
