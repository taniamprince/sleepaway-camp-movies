Kara's Sleepaway Camp Tribute

"Scream Queen" by Kara


I wanted to write to you to let you know I've been an avid follower of your website for quite some time. I've enjoyed the Sleepaway Camp series since I was just a little lass. I think it comes as a shock to most that a girl like me loves, endears, and has embraced this series so much. Its such a rare gem that not many people are aware of. Thanks to your site it has opened up the doors for more fans to come and enjoy the classic known as Sleepaway Camp.

I'm starting my own blog that will be dedicated to the entire horror genre. I'm a Scream Queen fanatic. I've always enjoyed Felissa Rose, Jamie Lee Curtis, Linnea Quigley, and Brinke Stevens. Hopefully one day I could possibly be up to their caliber.
